Clarity is a way of Life

British actor and director Harris Dickinson embodies the belief that imagination and risk-taking fuel growth — making him the face of the “Strive for Clarity” campaign.

Learn More

The Audi Concept C presents a concrete vision of what’s to come.

This is a first manifestation of our new design philosophy and an embodiment of our four design pillars: Clear, Technical, Intelligent, Emotional.

Reduction to the essential: The Audi Concept C embodies athletic minimalism with a purity of form, precision, and solidity.

A signature rear: Precisely defined volumes and a four-element light define the rear. Clean surfaces and horizontal slats underscore the car’s sporty character.

More than an aesthetic choice: Titanium stands for technical emotion, its satin lustre evokes a warm and technical elegance. A material that embodies precision, lightness, and strength.

A first: The Audi Concept C is the first Audi to feature a retractable hardtop. It combines the elegance of a coupé with an open-top experience.

Defined by geometric logic: The interior of the Audi Cocept C is both structured and emotionally resonant.
